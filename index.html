About Me

Hey there, My name is Stephon. 22 years of age. I'm a Web Designer, Front-End Developer and Animal lover based in Metro Detroit. I've always had a passion for creation. When I'm not up until 3 A.M punching codes, I'm usually sketching portraits or working on some other form of design work. I've made logo's, websites, illustrations and brochures for local businesses and a few up and comers as well. My love for creating has been a part of me since childhood, which has drawn me to the complexities of web development and the drive to continually improve my knowledge and skills. One of my defining traits is my ability to problem solve and think outside of the box model. I've worked as a operations manager and a SEO online specialist which has broadened my knowledge of the website trends all the cool kids are talking about.
